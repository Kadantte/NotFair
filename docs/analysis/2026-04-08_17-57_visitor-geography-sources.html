So what
  1. 1 Build SEO content targeting 'Google Ads MCP server', 'Google Ads AI agent', 'connect Google Ads to Claude' — Google organic is only 5 visitors in 14 days despite being the highest-intent channel (3 went directly to /connect or /audit). Even modest rankings could 3-5x organic traffic.
  2. 2 Double down on Reddit distribution — it drove 23 visitors (20% of all traffic) from a single launch post. Post weekly tips/case-studies to r/PPC, r/googleads, r/ClaudeAI. This is the only proven acquisition channel beyond your network.
  3. 3 Ignore international targeting for now — India (9 visitors) and other non-US markets show low engagement (2 pv/visitor). The WA-state cluster (13 visitors, 800+ pageviews) suggests product-market fit is local/US first. Google Ads is a US-centric product.
  4. 4 Tag all outbound links with UTM parameters — 54 'direct' visitors (47%) means you're flying blind on which channels work. Add utm_source to Reddit posts, email links, Twitter, and GitHub README.
