So what
  1. 1 Fix OAuth errors immediately — 3 production users hit PERMISSION_DENIED or Unknown error on /connect. These are users who already decided to try the product and were blocked. Check OAuth scope configuration and error handling. This is the highest-ROI fix because it converts already-motivated users.
  2. 2 Reduce homepage bounce (83%) — the landing page isn't compelling enough to drive clicks to /connect. Consider: (a) a stronger above-the-fold CTA, (b) a demo video or GIF showing the product in action, (c) social proof. With 47% mobile traffic, also consider whether the landing page communicates 'this is a desktop CLI tool' early enough to set expectations.
  3. 3 Investigate traffic sustainability — Apr 4 had 54 visitors (launch day), dropping to 5 and 2 on subsequent days. The email campaign (9 visitors) and Reddit posts (7) were one-time spikes. Need a repeatable acquisition channel (SEO for 'google ads mcp', content marketing, or paid).
